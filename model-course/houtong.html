2 Nights, 3 Days in Taiwan: A Tour of Cats and Abandoned Mines
Houtong Cat Village, Jiufen, Beitou Hot Springs Model Course

Taiwan, New Taipei City - Taipei ⏱ Read in 8 minutes 💰 Excellent cost-performance class 💑 Couples 👭 Girls' Trip

Houtong, just 35 minutes by train from Taipei, is a globally rare cat village where abandoned mines and stray cats coexist. Combining the fantastical lantern-lit night scenes of Jiufen and the sulfur hot springs of Beitou allows you to enjoy Taiwan's "hidden scenic spots" in a 2-night, 3-day trip. One of the attractions is the overwhelming cost performance, with transportation costs being just a few hundred yen round trip.

📌 Best Season

From October to April (Autumn, Winter, Spring) is the most comfortable. From May to September, there can be typhoons and extreme heat (over 35°C). The cat village operates year-round, and the cats are active even on rainy days.

📅 Model Course by Day

Day 1
Arrival in Taipei & Sunset in Jiufen
  • 14:00Check in at hotel in Taipei (the MRT Songshan-Chungxiao area is convenient)
  • 16:00Take the TRA (Taiwan Railways) to Ruifang Station (about 50 minutes from Taipei Station, NT$76〜)
  • 17:30🏮 Jiufen Old Street — The best time is at sunset when the lanterns are lit. Enjoy a tea ceremony experience at A-Mei Tea House, etc.
  • 20:00Return to Taipei from Ruifang Station and enjoy local gourmet food at Raohe Night Market (pepper buns, herbal rib stew)
Day 2
Houtong Cat Village & Jinguashi/Yin Yang Sea
  • 09:00Take the TRA to Houtong Station (about 35 minutes from Taipei Station, NT$38)
  • 09:45🐱 Houtong Cat Village — Interact freely with the cats on the bridge. Souvenir shops with cat goods are scattered around.
  • 11:30Move to Ruifang for lunch (local noodle shops and pork rice starting from ¥400)
  • 13:30Jinguashi — Tour the Gold Museum & the Japanese-style accommodation area.
  • 15:30🌊 Yin Yang Sea — A mystical inlet dyed greenish-blue by iron (a panoramic view from the observation deck).
  • 19:00Return to Taipei and have dinner at Shilin Night Market (large sausage wrapped in small sausage, big fried chicken, etc.)
Day 3
Beitou Hot Springs & Tamsui Old Street
  • 09:00Take the MRT to Beitou Hot Springs (about 30 minutes from Taipei Station)
  • 09:30♨️ Beitou Hot Springs Museum (free) & open-air foot bath (free) to warm up your feet first.
  • 11:00Day-use hot spring bath at a ryokan (starting from NT$500, towel rental available)
  • 13:00Move to Tamsui by MRT (approximately 30 minutes)
  • 13:30Lunch at Tamsui Old Street — Be sure to try the famous "Iron Eggs" and "A-Gei".
  • 15:30Stroll along sunset spots along the Tamsui River → return to Taipei Station and head home.

🏡 Recommended Accommodations

Using Taipei City (MRT Zhongxiao-Songshan area) as a base allows for efficient travel by train to Houtong, Jiufen, Beitou, and Tamsui. For great cost performance, business hotels in the Zhongxiao Fuxing to Nanjing Sanmin area are available from ¥5,000. If you want to splurge a little, there are also design hotels near Taipei that are Instagram-worthy.

💡 Transportation Cost Tips

The round trip from Taipei to Houtong is NT$76 (about ¥360). If you purchase an EasyCard (IC card), you get a 10% discount on fares. It is also useful for the airport MRT.
